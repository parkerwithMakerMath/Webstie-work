Who We Are

Math instruction built for the way kids actually learn

Maker Math is a one-on-one math tutoring program that replaces worksheets with 3D printers. We work with students from kindergarten through calculus — including kids who are struggling, bored, or differently wired with dyslexia, dyscalculia, or ADHD.

Every session is tailored to your child's level. We don't move on until a concept is truly mastered. And along the way, students build physical tools they designed themselves — tools that make math click in a way a textbook never could.

✎

Two sessions per week.
One student at a time.

In-person in Camp Hill, PA and Avon, NC — or fully online, with custom tools mailed right to your door.

Our Secret Weapon

3D Printing as a Teaching Tool

When a student designs and prints a triangle model that physically shows why angles add up to 180°, they don't just memorize it — they own it. Our 3D printing process transforms abstract concepts into objects you can hold, examine, and keep forever.

Students also absorb physics, engineering, and design thinking along the way — because when you're building real things, those subjects are impossible to separate from the math.

What We Offer

🎓

One-on-One Tutoring
